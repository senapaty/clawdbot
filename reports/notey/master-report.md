- Unique positioning (real instruments + gamification)
- Strong founder credibility (Grammy nominee + professor)
- Solid early traction (590% YoY growth)
- Healthy unit economics ($10 CAC, $60 LTV)
- 4 patent-pending technologies

**The Risks:**
- Yousician (‚Ç¨53M revenue) could copy features

## Final Assessment

**Grade: B+ (Strong potential, execution risk)**

Notey has a compelling product-market fit thesis with a credible founder and early traction. The combination of real instrument learning + gamification + kid-native design is genuinely differentiated. The B2B angle through schools is smart and defensible.

However, the competitive landscape is daunting. Yousician's resources and market position mean they could copy successful features. The founder's multiple commitments create execution risk. The niche market size limits upside potential.

**Recommendation:** Interesting opportunity for strategic investor who understands both gaming and education. Due diligence on B2B pipeline, actual retention metrics, and founder time commitment is critical. Consider leading or participating in the $1.5M round if terms are reasonable and B2B traction is verified.
